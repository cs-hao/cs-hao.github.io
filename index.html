Bio

 I am currently a second-year Ph.D student with the school of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Hefei University of Technology (HFUT), supervised by Prof. Zhong-Qiu Zhao. My research interests mainly focus on low-level vision including image super-resolution, image denoising, image dehazing, and model compression.
